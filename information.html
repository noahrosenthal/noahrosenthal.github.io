Background

I created NBAHoopsInsider website because I'm a passionate fan of basketball and the NBA. I wanted to create a platform where other fans like me could come together and stay up-to-date on the latest news, rumors, and insights from the world of basketball. With NBAHoopsInsider, I aim to provide a one-stop destination for all things NBA, including in-depth analysis, game scores, teams and players information and much more. By sharing my love of basketball with others, I hope to build a community of fans who can engage with each other and share their passion for the game.

My name is Noah Rosenthal, and I'm currently an undergraduate student at the University of Miami. My passion for basketball dates back to my childhood, when my grandfather introduced me to the sport. He spent countless hours rebounding for me while I practiced my shots, and instilled in me a love of the game that has stayed with me to this day. Basketball has been an integral part of my life ever since; when I wasn't in school, I was either playing or watching games with my friends. I remember them challenging me to 2v1 games because they couldn't beat me 1v1. My love for basketball continued to grow, and I played on my high school's varsity team for 3 and a half years, becoming the youngest player ever from my school to start on the team. Although my playing career came to an end after high school, my passion for the game remained strong. I began analyzing and watching games more closely, and as a fan of the Boston Celtics, I watched all 82 games every season as well as the playoffs. I wanted to share my thoughts and connect with other fans like me, who love the game but can't play at the NBA level. That's why I started my journey at NBAHoopsInsider.

Information

As a platform, our main goal is to provide our customers with the latest breaking news and information on all players and teams. However, we don't just want to stop there. Our vision for the future is to expand our platform in numerous ways to give our fans an unparalleled experience. Firstly, we want our fans to be able to see scores and watch highlights of each game, which we believe will be a key part of the website. We will work towards receiving approval from the NBA, but that will not deter us from bringing this feature to our fans. Another exciting feature we hope to implement is to hire journalists and basketball analysts to write original NBAHoopsInsider articles, instead of relying on external links to pages such as ESPN and BleacherReport. This will enable us to attract customers away from our competitors and bring them to our website. We also want to incorporate player interviews, as interaction is even better than just reading news and analysis. We envision a place on the website where customers can submit questions for an NBA player to answer, creating a unique relationship between players and fans. This is crucial because we believe that at the end of the day, NBA players are not just athletes, they are fathers, sons, brothers, and husbands before NBA players. Our platform aims to break down the misconception that they are just basketball players. Finally, we want to create an interactive page where users can play games, take quizzes to test their basketball knowledge, and participate in polls. With a whole new generation consuming media in a different way than before, interactivity is more important than ever. By providing upcoming game predictions, quizzes on basketball history, and polls on which player is better, we aim to keep our fans engaged and excited about basketball.
